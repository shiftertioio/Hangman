How to play the Hangman game?

Player 2 will first set a word that they want Player 1 to guess.
Player 2 will then press the "Enter Word" button to signify that Player 1 can start guessing.

If Player 1 gets a wrong letter, Player 2 will press the "Wrong Letter" button.
The hangman will advance by a stage. There are 6 stages in total.

Repeat the above till the player guesses the word, in which, press the green "Word Guessed" button.
If player fails to guess the word before the GG hangman stage, they loose the game.

Click the yellow "Reveal Word" button to reveal the word hidden.
Press the black "End game" button to restart the game.

End of instructions
